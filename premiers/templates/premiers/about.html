The experience of working since he was 10 years old to earn his own pocket money caused Kajang natives, Mohd Ali Abdul Majis or Ali Majis to not sit still. Mohd Ali who is also the Chief Executive Officer of ALIY Property Advisors Sdn Bhd is aware that the family background of living a modest life requires him to work hard to continuing his survival.

Alhamdulillah, my father's education and firmness all this time caused me to become a 'person'. Since the age of 13, I have been using my own money to fund my studies. “My father told me to be independent because I failed to get excellent results in the Primary School Assessment Test (UPSR) because I worked more. As a child, I like to earn my own money because I want to own toys, ”he said.

But everything changed when Mohd Ali studied at a private institution of higher learning and did not receive financial loan financing.

"I can not afford to continue my studies and decide to do any job that allows me to have money. “Alhamdulillah, I was given the opportunity to learn again, at the same time being the owner of a car wash. I believe sustenance is everywhere, ”he said. Mohd Ali is grateful for being given the opportunity to learn again and his business provides lucrative profits. “Until I was attacked by 30 people while in the area around my business premises. I was hacked and felt this world was dark and thought I was no longer in this world.

“Alhamdulillah, there was a servant of GOD helping me and I was rushed to the hospital. I appreciate this second opportunity and promise to strive to change my life for the better, ”he said. After recovering, Mohd Ali was banned from working and was only given the trust to work in his uncle's company located in Kuala Lumpur. Despite not having a formal education in real estate, Mohd Ali managed to buy and sell his first property and received a commission of RM1,000.

“I have no experience working in the office because all this time I have been doing various jobs that use physical strength. I was given the opportunity to experience various types of jobs in the company until the last position was sales manager, giving me the opportunity to meet people. "Since then, I have just opened my eyes to the real estate field and did not turn around and get involved in the real estate auction," he said. Mohd Ali wisely planned his life when he was able to generate an income of more than RM100,000 despite having a fixed salary of only RM4,000.

Currently, Mohd Ali is a developer and plans to develop housing and commercial building projects in Sungai Besi, Kuala Lumpur and Sungai Udang, Melaka.

Xera Scheduling is a booking appointment app exclusively for Aliy Property Advisors Sdn Bhd client. It always displays and recommends available time slots so that client can have efficient booking appointment session. Xera Scheduling play role as a Human Personal Assistant to help client by giving the best recommendation free slot for the client and schedule meeting.

Usually, the assistant needs more time to complete the scheduling process. Before proposing an empty slot with the best recommendation, he needs to check the boss’s calendar then find empty slot to make sure there is no redundant booking. To provide the best recommendation slot, assistant need to follow the concept of “Prioritize the main ones”. He needs to evaluate various aspects for example, who is representative of client’s company, what company the client represents, how much potential and urgent of meeting purpose.

The solution, Xera helps speed up the evaluation process by classifying slots into 3 classes, namely Glory, Master and Elite bases on all the information provided by client. Based on those classes, Xera provides the best slots recommendation and allocates to the specific Aliy Company representative during the meeting. Xera helps facilitate assistant in terms of evaluation and scheduling.
